Pizza

Fresh out of the coal oven, Neopolitan Pizza

Pizza - it's a classic. This easy meal not only tatses great, but the cost per serving is very low. An Italian
marvel loved by the entire world, pizza is universally known, and thanks to this recipe, is easier to make than ever!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Mozzarella
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Toppings

Steps

  1. Toss and spread dough
  2. Using a large spoon, spread tomatoe sauce on top of dough evenly
  3. Sprinkle mozzarella cheese on top of tomatoe sauce evenly
  4. Add toppings as desired. This part doesn't have to be even!
  5. Bake in over for 15 minutes at 400 degrees Farenheit
  6. Use pizza cutter to make 4-8 slices. Serve
