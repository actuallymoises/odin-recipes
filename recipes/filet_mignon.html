Filet Mignon is one of the most exquisite dishes. The name may not be easy to pronounce, this is because the name is in French. Filet
mignon is so desirable because it is so tender. The filet practically melts in your mouth!

Ingredients

  • Filet Mignon
  • Rosemary
  • Salt and Pepper
  • Garlic
  • Avocado Oil
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Prep the steaks
  2. Sear the steaks
  3. Add the butter, garlic and rosemary
  4. Bake the steaks
  5. Cover the steaks
  6. Serve with veggies and starches
