Spaghetti

Spaghetti is served!

Spaghetti is a very simple dish to chef up, and it is quite filling and therefore a satisfying dish for you and your guests.
This highly customizable dish makes it quite versatile, and can be enjoyed in many ways. Be sure to add your own twist! No pun intended...

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti Noodles
  • Tomatoe Sauce
  • Olive Oil
  • Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Boil spaghetti for 10 minutes. Add a pinch of salt
  2. Heat sauce in a pan, season with olive oil
  3. Combine pasta and sauce
  4. Add salt and pepper. Serve
